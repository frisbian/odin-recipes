Garlic Bread

Garlic bread by nikhil bali

This is a simple Garlic Bread recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of french bread or similiar, halved.
  • 4 Cloves of garlic, minced or crushed
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 °F
  2. Soften butter in microwave for 20-40 seconds
  3. Mix in garlic, parmesan, salt and pepper.
  4. Brush butter mixture over both sides of bread.
  5. Bake for 8-12 minutes or until desired doneness.
  6. Top with parsley and cut into pieces.
